Hello and welcome to the Voron3d wiki

 

the following are my hopes for this project:

  • have a easily searchable collection of voron modifications alone with the links to their respective github/other links.
  • I want to try to keep track of compatibility in-between different mods to help the community find out information more easily.
  • attempt to keep a record of the knowledge on discord as a lot of the 3d printing community uses discord servers, which can be hard to search and difficult to read
  • keep track of some of the history of the voron project and it's origins

 

more updates coming soon
