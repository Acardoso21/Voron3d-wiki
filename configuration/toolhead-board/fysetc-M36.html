Fysetc M36 Toolhead Board

Overview

The Fysetc M36 Toolhead Board is a specialized breakout board designed for use in CoreXY 3D printers, including Voron and other high-performance machines. This board consolidates various toolhead-related connections, making wiring cleaner and reducing the number of cables routed through the printer's moving parts.

Features

  • Compact Design: Small form factor makes it easy to mount directly to the toolhead.
  • Simplified Wiring: Reduces cable management issues by centralizing connections to a single board.
  • Support for Multiple Sensors and Devices: Compatible with thermistors, hotends, fans, and extruders.
  • Standardized Connectors: Uses JST-XH connectors for easy plug-and-play integration with components.
  • Compatible with 24V Systems: Designed to work with 24V-powered machines.

Installation

  1. Mount the Board: Use the mounting holes to attach the M36 Toolhead Board to the toolhead carriage of your printer.
  2. Connect Components: Plug the thermistor, fans, heater, and other components into the appropriate JST-XH connectors on the M36 board.
  3. Wire to Mainboard: Route the consolidated wiring from the toolhead back to the mainboard. This reduces cable bulk and simplifies maintenance.
  4. Configure Firmware: Adjust your firmware settings to ensure proper communication with the M36 board, especially for temperature sensors and fan control.

Supported Firmware

  • Klipper: Minimal configuration changes are required to integrate the M36 with Klipper firmware. Just ensure that the thermistor type, heater settings, and fan control pins are correctly configured.
  • Marlin: Use standard Marlin configuration files to assign the correct pin numbers for each function (thermistors, fans, heaters, etc.).
