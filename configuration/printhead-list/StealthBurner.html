StealthBurner

The Voron StealthBurner is an upgraded toolhead for Voron 3D printers, designed to improve performance, cooling, and aesthetics. It integrates a dual-fan cooling system, optimizing airflow for both the hotend and printed parts, ensuring better cooling during high-speed prints. This results in reduced print defects like stringing and warping. It also features RGB LEDs, providing real-time feedback on printer status and temperature changes, adding functionality and visual appeal. Overall, the StealthBurner enhances both the performance and appearance of Voron printers, making it ideal for users seeking high-quality, reliable 3D printing.
